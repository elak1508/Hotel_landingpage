Room Types

Single Occupency Room

Single room occupancy (SRO) is a type of rental housing that consists of small, furnished rooms that are shared with communal facilities like bathrooms and kitchens.

Family Room

A family room that's 12 ft by 18 ft can comfortably accommodate a family of six to ten people, depending on the furniture. However, the ideal floor plan for your family room can be customized to suit your needs and family size

Duplex Room

It's a two-level room. It consists of at least one bedroom on one floor and a living room or bedroom on the second floor. You can also find more bedrooms on any of the floors. These types of rooms are commonly chosen by families with small children who go to bed much earlier, and thanks to two levels they are provided with more comfort and silence.
